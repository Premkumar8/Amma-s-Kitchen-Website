Shipping & Delivery Policy

1. Service Area

Currently, Amma’s Kitchen delivers exclusively within Coimbatore and surrounding areas. We do not ship internationally.

2. Delivery Timing
  • Orders placed before 12:00 PM: Delivered on the same day (Subject to availability).
  • Orders placed after 12:00 PM: Delivered the next business day.
  • Standard Delivery Time: 1 - 2 Business Days.
3. Shipping Charges

Shipping costs are calculated based on the delivery location and order value:

  • Standard Delivery Fee: ₹30
  • Free Delivery: On orders above ₹500.
4. Delays

Amma’s Kitchen is not liable for any delay in delivery by the courier company / postal authorities and only guarantees to hand over the consignment to the courier company or postal authorities within 1 working day from the date of the order and payment or as per the delivery date agreed at the time of order confirmation.

5. Contact Us

If your order is delayed beyond 2 days, please contact us immediately at +91 8220488716.
